Savory Cheeseburgers

Description

Imagine sinking your teeth into a juicy, perfectly grilled cheeseburger. The melty cheese oozes over the savory beef patty, complemented by fresh lettuce, ripe tomato, and a soft, toasted bun. This classic cheeseburger recipe is a timeless favorite, perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a weekend barbecue. Simple to make and incredibly satisfying, it promises to be a hit with everyone at the table. Enjoy the delicious layers of flavor in every bite.

Ingredients (Serves 5)

  • Ground Beef: 1.5 pounds of ground beef (preferably 80/20 for juiciness).
  • Cheese: 5 slices of cheddar cheese (or your favorite cheese).
  • Lettuce: 5 leaves of fresh lettuce.
  • Tomato: 1 large tomato, sliced.
  • Onion: 1 small onion, sliced (optional).
  • Pickles: Sliced pickles (optional).
  • Condiments: Ketchup, mustard, mayo (to taste).
  • Seasoning: Salt and pepper.
  • Buns: 5 hamburger buns.

Instructions

  1. Divide the ground beef into 5 equal portions.
  2. Shape each portion into a patty, about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Season both sides of each patty with salt and pepper.
  4. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  6. Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  7. Lightly butter the insides of the hamburger buns.
  8. Toast the buns on the grill or in a skillet until golden brown.
  9. Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom half of each toasted bun.
  10. Add a cooked patty with melted cheese on top of the lettuce.
  11. Top with a slice of tomato, onion, and pickles if using.
  12. Spread your favorite condiments on the top half of the bun.

Your cheeseburger is ready to be devoured! Enjoy the classic flavors and satisfaction of a homemade burger.
